# FLYRA — Brand Book v1.0

## Brand Identity

**Name:** FLYRA — صعود (Ascend)
**Tagline:** "ASCEND BEYOND" — صعود بلا حدود
**Origin:** Algeria 🇩🇿
**Launch:** 2026
**Vibe:** The sky has no ceiling. FLYRA is for those who move differently — blending Old Money elegance with streetwear edge, rooted in Algerian heritage, built for the world.

## Logo Concept
A stylized bird in flight — minimalist line art, suggesting upward movement and freedom. Use the bird alone (icon) or with "FLYRA" wordmark below.

**Icon:** 🐦 (text fallback)
**Wordmark colors:** Midnight #0A0F1E with sky-blue accent

## Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Midnight | #0A0F1E | Primary background, logo |
| Sky | #7BA3C9 | Primary accent, links |
| Amber | #C49B3D | Premium highlights, badges |

| Sunset | #D4956A | Warm accents, sale tags |

## Typography
- **Display:** Inter (900 weight) — bold, modern, accessible
- **Body:** Inter (400/500) — clean readability
- **Arabic:** Noto Sans Arabic — for RTL support
- **Taglines:** Letter-spacing: 0.15em, uppercase, thin weight

## Brand Voice
- Confident but not arrogant
- Algerian pride, global ambition
- "We make climbing easy" — premium quality, accessible price
- Heritage + futurism

## Visual Style
- Deep, moody backgrounds with light text
- Amber/gold accents for premium feel
- Clean geometric layouts
- Subtle gradients (sky blue → midnight)
- Bird motifs subtly integrated

## Slogan Variations

2. "Rise Without Limits"
3. "From Algeria to the World"
4. "The Sky Has No Ceiling"

## Sub-Branches / Collections
- **FLYRA HERITAGE** — Algerian-inspired modern wear (Gandoura reimagined, traditional patterns)
- **FLYRA STREET** — Urban streetwear (oversized, graphics, bold)
- **FLYRA OLD MONEY** — Quiet luxury (neutral tones, premium fabrics, timeless cuts)
- **FLYRA SPORT** — Activewear (performance fabrics, sleek design)
- **FLYRA Hijab Edit** — Modest fashion (stylish, comfortable)

## Price Positioning
- Mid-to-premium: 8,000–95,000 DZD
- Quality over quantity
- Free shipping over 25,000 DZD
- COD available across Algeria

## Target Audience
- Algerian youth (18-35) who care about style
- Want something different from generic fast fashion
- Proud of Algerian identity, open to global trends
- Active on Instagram, TikTok
- Use CCP/baridimob for payments
